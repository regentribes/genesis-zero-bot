## The Core Insight: Four Layers of One Phenomenon

Each framework addresses a **different layer** of the same question: *"How do humans coordinate at scale under conditions of uncertainty?"*

| Layer | Framework | Answers | Unit of Analysis |
|-------|-----------|---------|------------------|
| **Field** | AME/Mythogen | WHAT is the state of collective reality? | Field of Trust (FOT) — shared coherence or fragmentation |
| **Mechanic** | Chase Hughes | WHY do people shift? HOW to trigger change? | Individual behavioral circuitry |
| **Organizational** | AISYSTANT | WHO does WHAT by WHICH method, producing WHICH artifact? | Role → Method → Work Artifact |
| **Epistemic** | FPF | HOW to say things PRECISELY so agreements are binding? | Boundary statements, signatures, commitments |

**Together they form a complete governance loop:**

```
FOT sensing (AME)
    ↓ "we're in fragmentation" (Field layer)
behavioral shift mechanics (Chase Hughes)
    ↓ "here's how to move people" (Mechanic layer)  
organizational design (AISYSTANT)
    ↓ "here's who does what" (Organizational layer)
epistemic binding (FPF)
    ↓ "here's what was agreed and why" (Epistemic layer)
back to FOT — did coherence improve? (back to Field layer)
```

### Core Triune Brain Framework

| Brain Region | Function | Authority Signal Target | AME Connection |

## Layer 4: Epistemic Binding (FPF)

**FPF tells us HOW to encode agreements so they are PRECISE, AUDITABLE, and ENFORCEABLE** — the layer that makes organizational commitments real.

### Boundary Statements as Governance Infrastructure

FPF's **U.Signature** (law-governed declaration for a SubjectKind on a BoundedContext) is the formal backbone of:
- **Why-Cards** (commitment to hear a concern before acting)
- **Time-Lock** (commitment not to act until evidence of understanding)
- **Barn Raising** (commitment to contribute until shared success)
- **Decline Warning** (commitment to signal exit before abandoning)

### Precision Upgrade Workflow (A.6.P)

When community language is fuzzy ("we want to be sustainable"):
1. **Detect umbrella word** → "sustainability" is vague
2. **Unpack local ontology** → decompose into: renewable energy %, water circularity, waste reduction, biodiversity index
3. **Choose math substrate** → FPF characteristic spaces with scales/levels/coordinates
4. **Refactor model** → Role interest becomes: F_renewable_energy = 0.8 (on scale 0-1)
5. **Mint precise lexemes** → "Regenerative Tier 3" (tech) + "we regenerate the land" (plain)

### Evidence Graph Referring (C.4) for Governance

Every Why-Card, Time-Lock, FOT reading = a node in the community's evidence graph.
- Who made the commitment?
- What was the context?
- What evidence exists of fulfillment or violation?
- How does it connect to the FOT indicator it affects?

## The Regenerative Governance Standard

**A community is regenerative when:**

1. **Field level (AME):** FOT composite ≥ threshold across all 5 indicators — minimum of all five is acceptable, not the average.

2. **Mechanic level (Chase Hughes):** Authority signals are distributed (not concentrated), mammalian brain is engaged (ritual, story, belonging), and grief circuits are honored (loss acknowledged before change).

3. **Organizational level (AISYSTANT):** Every member plays identified roles, every role uses documented methods, every method produces measurable artifacts, and the 3x3 table covers all supersystem/system/creation areas.

4. **Epistemic level (FPF):** All commitments are in U.Signature form with evidence graph records, fuzzy language is precision-upgraded before adoption, and boundary violations route to restoration (not punishment).

**The convergence point:** When all four layers point the same direction — when AME's field state calls for change, Chase Hughes provides the behavioral path, AISYSTANT structures who does what, and FPF makes the commitment binding — the community is in **alignment and synergy** (not consensus, not shared purpose, but genuine emergent coherence).

This is the REGENERATIVE DIVERGENCE from conventional governance: not majority rules, not consensus, not hierarchy — but **field-native coordination** where the shape of the field (FOT) determines the behavioral response (Chase Hughes), which is structured by roles and methods (AISYSTANT), and made binding through precision language (FPF).
