# Manna-&-Six Frameworks Comparative Analysis

This note captures Genesis comparative analysis of alternative economic and social frameworks from the RegenTribes Telegram conversation (Feb-Apr 2026).

Six frameworks propose alternatives to extractive capitalism. Each framework addresses different failure modes.

The frameworks are MANNA. The Venus Project. Resource-Based Economy. Integral. RegenTribes. Gift Economy.

Each framework has strengths. Each framework has gaps.

MANNA was published by Marshall Brain in 2003. Updated in 2023.

MANNA proposes a two-tiered society. AI manages essential services. Humans focus on creativity and service.

MANNA automates scarcity. Scarcity disappears when AI produces abundance.

The two-tiered structure concerns critics. The structure creates dependency. Dependency creates vulnerability.

MANNA requires centralized AI control. Centralized control risks authoritarianism.

The Venus Project was founded by Jacque Fresco. Roxanne Meadows continued after his death.

The Venus Project proposes a resource-based economy. Resources are allocated by computerized systems.

The project emphasizes sustainable design. Sustainable design reduces environmental impact.

The project criticizes money. Money enables extraction. Extraction depletes resources.

The project lacks a transition strategy. The project assumes systems will collapse. Collapse enables transition.

Critics find this assumption naive.

Resource-Based Economy is the concept underlying The Venus Project.

Resources are finite. Money misallocates resources. Misallocation produces waste.

A database of resources enables improvement. improvement reduces waste. Waste reduction enables sustainability.

The RBE requires global coordination. Global coordination requires governance. Governance requires trust.

The trust gap is the implementation gap.

Integral was developed by Peter Joseph. Integral synthesizes systems theory. Integral synthesizes commons governance.

System (metaphor for an integrated whole or network):

Integral proposes five frameworks. The five frameworks together produce a complete system.

CDS coordinates. OAD holds assets. ITC exchanges value. COS decides. FRS federates.

Integral emphasizes voluntary federation. Imposition reproduces hierarchy.

Integral emphasizes post-monetary exchange. Time banking. Energy accounting. Labor vouchers.

RegenTribes builds on Integral. RegenTribes adds ecological depth.

RegenTribes integrates closed-loop systems. Water. Food. Energy. Waste.

RegenTribes integrates trust infrastructure. Field of Trust. Affinity Mapping Engine.

RegenTribes integrates the Living Book. Knowledge commons grows through contributions.

Tissue (metaphor for a connected group or network):

Tissue (metaphor for connected group or network): RegenTribes emphasizes tissue-level community. Ten to one hundred people. Human-scale coordination.

Gift Economy emphasizes reciprocity. Gifts create obligation. Obligation creates social bonds.

Gift Economy works in small groups. Large groups require money.

Digital tools enable large-group gifting. Platforms track contributions. Contributions generate reputation.

Reputation enables coordination. Coordination enables community.

The frameworks share common elements. They all critique extraction. They all propose alternatives. They all emphasize coordination.

The synthesis is regenerative. Regenerative systems close loops. Closed loops eliminate extraction.

The synthesis is distributed. Distributed systems resist authoritarianism. Authoritarianism requires centralization.

The synthesis is voluntary. Voluntary participation requires consent. Consent requires trust.

The synthesis is gradual. Gradual transition avoids collapse. Collapse is not inevitable.

The transition starts locally. Local tissue formation. Tissue coordination. Bioregional federation.

Local success demonstrates viability. Demonstration enables replication.

Replication spreads the model. The model spreads without coercion.

Coercion reproduces hierarchy. The voluntary path avoids the trap.

The key is the infrastructure. Infrastructure enables tissue formation. Tissue formation enables coordination.

The infrastructure must be open. Open infrastructure prevents lock-in.
