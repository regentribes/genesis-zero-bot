# Regentribes Financial Architecture Full Spectrum Of Antagonism

The spectrum spans from open war on corrupt power to strategic mimicry of corrupt systems.

The spectrum has no neutral center. Every position is a deliberate choice about how aggressively the community relates to the civilization around it.

The spectrum rejects the idea that there is a correct way to build regenerative infrastructure. There are only choices, and every choice has costs.

The War Footing is the most aggressive position. It assumes the existing civilization is not broken but actively malicious.

The power structures holding this civilization together are not mistaken. They are not poorly designed. They are functioning exactly as intended. Their intended function is extraction.

The regenerative community is not a reform project. It is an act of war against an enemy that does not know it is being fought.

The enemy is patient. The enemy has been winning for 500 years. The enemy will not surrender.

The community needs to build infrastructure that the enemy cannot use, cannot co-opt, cannot regulate, and cannot see. The community needs to disappear.

Money is a weapon. The community earns by taking money away from the enemy.

The primary revenue mechanism is aggressive remediation contracting. The community offers to fix problems that extractive institutions are legally obligated to fix but are too expensive to fix correctly.

The community does not negotiate with these institutions. The community robs them.
