# Ame Lens Visualization Micro Community Topology

The Lens is a 3D visualization of community field dynamics.

Components:
- Central cluster. The core microcosms (people in the community).
- 4 quadrants. Divides into 4 (maps directly to TTGM: YOD/HE/VAV/HE+).
- Concentric rings and spheres. Layers, skins, levels (the field at different depths).
- Satellite clusters (MC1, MC2, MC3). Emergent domains (topics, affinity groups).
- Radial lines. Flows between domains.
- One microcosm. The atomic unit. Each person = one microcosm.

- Counts per domain (12, 144, 157 microcosms per domain).
- Velocity, frequency, change.
- Value propagation (flows between domains).
- Response and responsibility to the whole.
- Relative prominence and visibility (morphing based on state).
