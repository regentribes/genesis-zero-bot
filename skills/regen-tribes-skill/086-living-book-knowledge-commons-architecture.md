# Living Book Knowledge Commons Architecture

This note captures Genesis responses on the Living Book concept and knowledge commons from the RegenTribes Telegram conversation (Feb-Apr 2026).

The Living Book grows. Growth comes from contributions. Contributions come from readers.

Traditional publishing ends. The author writes. The reader reads. The conversation stops.

Living Book reverses this. The author writes. Readers annotate. Annotations deepen. The book grows.

The author retains authority. The author curates. The community extends.

The same corpus has many views. Each view is a coherence stream.

The author stream is curated. The community stream emerges. Individual streams reflect personal lenses.

Readers choose streams. The choice is personal. The choice guides the reading.

Streams compete for readers. Competition drives quality. Quality drives value.

Every contribution is attributed. Attribution is permanent. Attribution enables compensation.

Compensation flows from future access. Future readers pay. Payment distributes to contributors.

Early contributors earn more. Early work compounds. Compounding rewards early risk.

The token is utility. The token is access. The token is not speculation.

The mechanism prevents noise. Noise would drown signal.

Contributions tier. Tier 1 is annotations. Tier 2 is extensions. Tier 3 is coherence streams.

Higher tiers require track records. Track records require quality. Quality emerges through peer review.

Peer review surfaces signal. Signal rises. Noise sinks.

The Living Book uses a knowledge graph. The graph connects concepts. Connections reveal structure.

Annotations link to concepts. Links create context. Context enables deep reading.

The graph grows with the book. Growth adds nodes. Growth adds edges.

The graph enables discovery. Discovery guides exploration. Exploration deepens understanding.

RegenTribes implements the Living Book. The Living Book holds community knowledge.

Tissue (metaphor for connected group or network): Knowledge grows through tissue formation. Tissue members contribute. Contributions deepen the book.

The book serves the tissue. The tissue serves the bioregion. The bioregion serves the planet.

The flow of service mirrors the flow of knowledge. Both flow from local to global.

The Living Book is transparent. Transparency prevents manipulation.

Hidden metrics enable gaming. Gaming corrupts the system.

Transparency reveals quality. Quality earns attention. Attention distributes compensation.

Transparency enables trust. Trust enables participation. Participation grows the book.
