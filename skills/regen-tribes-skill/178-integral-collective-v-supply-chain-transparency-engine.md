# Integral Collective V Supply Chain Transparency Engine

System (metaphor for an integrated whole or network):

This architecture builds a transparency system where every product carries its complete history.
Consumers verify impact before purchase.
Regenerative practices are visible and rewarded.
Circular economy is enforced automatically.

Fusion 1 is UNTP DPPs with IOTA Stardust.
Digital Product Passports on feeless ledger.
Every product has verifiable provenance record.
No transaction fees enable fine-grained tracking.

Fusion 2 is RWA tokenization with DPPs.
Each product is a fractional token.
Ownership verifiable on-chain.
Revenue shares flow automatically to producers.

Fusion 3 is IOTA Smart Contracts with circular rules.
End-of-life disposal mandated in smart contract.
Disposal triggers material recovery payment.
Virgin resource extraction becomes expensive.

Fusion 4 is ZK proofs with conformity credentials.
Anonymous verification of compliance.
Producers prove sustainability without revealing business secrets.
Consumers verify claims without seeing full supply chain.

Credential 1 is the Digital Product Passport.
Issued by producer at creation.
Records origin, materials, process.
Updates at each lifecycle event.

Credential 2 is the Digital Traceability Event.
Records make, move, modify events.
Mass-balance verification at facilities.
Full upstream traceability linked.

Credential 3 is the Conformity Credential.
Issued by independent assessor.
Verifies compliance with regenerative standards.
Includes accreditation and reference scheme.

Credential 4 is the Identity Anchor.
Links digital identity to regulatory registry.
Binds physical entity to digital credentials.
Verifiable by any party.

Flow 1 is the creation flow.
New regenerative product enters system.
DPP minted with origin credentials.
Fractional tokens distributed to producers.
Impact tokens minted for founding contributions.

Flow 2 is the exchange flow.
Product moves through supply chain.
Each transfer updates DTE.
Payment streams via x402 or IOTA.
Ownership fractions maintained on ledger.

Flow 3 is the consumption flow.
Consumer purchases product.
DPP verified before purchase.
ZK proofs confirm sustainability claims.
Payment settles to all fractional owners.

Flow 4 is the end-of-life flow.
Consumer disposes product.
QR code links to DPP.
Smart contract verifies disposal obligation.
Recovery payment flows to recycler.
New raw materials enter creation flow.

Layer 1 is the origin layer.
Producer credentials verified.
Location matches claimed origin.
Raw material sources documented.

Layer 2 is the process layer.
Conformity assessor validates process.
Labor practices meet community standards.
Environmental impact within limits.

Layer 3 is the transport layer.
Chain of custody verified.
Energy used in transport calculated.
Carbon footprint added to DPP.

Layer 4 is the consumption layer.
Product authenticity verified.
Sustainability claims proven via ZK.
Impact of purchase shown to consumer.

Effect 1 is price transparency.
Full cost visible in price.
Externalities internalized.
Sustainable products competitive with conventional.

Effect 2 is provenance premium.
Consumers pay more for verified impact.
Regenerative producers earn premium.
Transition to regenerative practices profitable.

Effect 3 is circular incentives.
Disposal cost born by producer.
Recycling becomes revenue.
Virgin extraction expensive.

Effect 4 is trust emergence.
Trustless verification replaces trust in brands.
Verification is cryptographic.
Fraud becomes impossible.

Implementation 1 is IOTA Stardust deployment.
Single Stardust network for all communities.
Cross-community trade enabled.
UNTP credentials recognized across networks.

Implementation 2 is x402 integration.
Micro-transactions for material tracking.
Granular payment splits across owners.
AI agents manage logistics autonomously.

Implementation 3 is ZK proof issuance.
Circom circuits for conformity proofs.
Provers run on producer hardware.
Verifiers run on consumer devices.

Implementation 4 is community adoption.
Communities define regenerative standards.
Standards encoded as UNTP conformity criteria.
Gradual rollout builds trust.
