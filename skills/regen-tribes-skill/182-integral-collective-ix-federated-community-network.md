# Integral Collective Ix Federated Community Network

This architecture enables communities to federate while maintaining sovereignty.
Each community runs its own stack.
Cross-community interactions are seamless.
No community dominates another.

Fusion 1 is Holochain federation with cross-network DHT.
Communities run separate Holochain networks.
DHT bridges connect communities.
Source chains cross-reference without merging.

Fusion 2 is Solid POD federation with WebID.
Members have PODs in their community.
Cross-community access via Solid protocols.
No centralized identity provider.

Fusion 3 is ICP canisters with cross-chain bridges.
Resource trades between communities execute on ICP.
Canisters hold cross-community agreements.
IOTA bridges connect ledgers.

Fusion 4 is UNTP with cross-community credentials.
Regenerative standards recognized across communities.
Conformity credentials verified by any community.
No single community sets all standards.

Layer 1 is the identity layer.
WebIDs span communities.
ZK proofs verify community membership.
Identity stays local, verification is portable.

Layer 2 is the data layer.
Solid PODs federate across communities.
Data sharing via consent protocols.

Layer 3 is the governance layer.
Communities maintain sovereignty.
Cross-community proposals use standard format.
No super-governance overrides local decisions.

Layer 4 is the economic layer.
x402 enables cross-community payments.
IOTA bridges transfer value.
Resource flows without currency conversion.
