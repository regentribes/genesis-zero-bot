The Integral Parallel Economy is a meta-regenerative economic framework.

The word integral means it addresses all four quadrants of human systems: individual interior, individual exterior, collective interior, collective exterior.

The word parallel means it operates alongside the existing monetary economy rather than requiring its collapse as a precondition.

The word economy means it addresses the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services at a systems level.

Regenerative communities typically focus on regenerating specific domains: land, water, food systems, or social fabric.

The Integral Parallel Economy regenerates the economic substrate itself.

Without a functioning economic substrate, all other regenerative work eventually collapses or gets captured.

The monetary system extracts regenerative value and concentrates it in private hands.

The Integral Parallel Economy creates a parallel system that channels regenerative value back to communities.

The Integral Parallel Economy has five interconnected subsystems.

Subsystem 1 is the Time Currency System.
This is a transparent, demurrage-based complementary currency measured in hours of labor.
Labor-hour accounting makes exchange value objective and auditable.
Demurrage ensures currency circulation rather than hoarding.

Subsystem 2 is the Transparent Scope Protocol.
This is a peer-to-peer verification system where participants validate each other's contributions.
The protocol replaces corporate auditing with mutual accountability.
It creates trust without centralized authority.

Subsystem 3 is the Resource Distribution Network.
This is a demand-aggregated procurement system that eliminates waste through collective planning.
Producers receive guaranteed purchase commitments before production begins.
This eliminates the boom-bust cycle of conventional production.

Body (metaphor for the complete collective or organism):

Subsystem 4 is the Integral Governance Assembly.
This is a consent-based decision-making body that governs the parallel economy.
It operates on sociocratic principles with nested circles.
No individual or faction can override the consent of those affected by a decision.

Subsystem 5 is the Regenerative Investment Fund.
This is a community-controlled capital allocation system directing surplus toward regenerative projects.
Investment decisions are made by the community governance assembly.
Returns are distributed as community credits rather than private profit.

The economy does not require dismantling the existing monetary system to function.

Citizens participate in both systems simultaneously.

Monetary income continues to flow for external transactions and legal compliance.

Parallel economy value supplements and eventually replaces extracted monetary value.

This avoids the collapse risk that has destroyed every previous attempt at alternative economics.

The Integral Parallel Economy addresses five interconnected domains.

Domain 1 is financial regeneration. Community capital is accumulated, allocated, and distributed through transparent mechanisms. No extraction to external shareholders. Interest-bearing debt is replaced by time currency demurrage.

Domain 2 is ecological regeneration. Production is designed for biological cycle closure. Waste becomes input for another process. Measurement is through genuine progress indicators, not GDP.

Domain 3 is social regeneration. Relationships are structured through consent and mutual accountability. Power concentration is prevented through nested governance. Isolation is replaced by genuine community belonging.

Domain 4 is knowledge regeneration. Expertise is shared freely through the community knowledge base. Education is continuous and non-competitive. Knowledge accumulates for collective benefit rather than private advantage.

Domain 5 is cultural regeneration.
Stories and symbols support the regenerative worldview.
Cultural production serves community cohesion rather than market extraction.
Art and music emerge from community practice rather than entertainment industry.

The Integral Parallel Economy is unique among alternative economic proposals in five ways.

Unique 1 is parallel operation. Every other alternative economic proposal requires the existing system to be dismantled first. The Integral Parallel Economy can begin now, operating alongside the existing system.

Unique 2 is integral design. Every other proposal addresses one or two quadrants. The Integral Parallel Economy addresses all four: individual, collective, interior, exterior.

Unique 3 is failure mode correction. The Integral Parallel Economy was designed by studying every previous attempt and correcting its specific failure mode.

Unique 4 is technology integration.
The Integral Parallel Economy is designed for technology-native implementation from the start.
It uses P2P networks, verifiable credentials, and autonomous agents rather than treating them as add-ons.

Unique 5 is community adaptability.
The framework is designed to be instantiated by any community regardless of local context.
Each community implements the five subsystems according to its own culture and constraints.
