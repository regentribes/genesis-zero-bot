## Writing Standard: ASD-STE100 Issue 9

Descriptive sentences: maximum 25 words.
Prescriptive or procedural sentences: maximum 20 words.
Active voice. Present tense only.
One line equals one sentence or one fragment.
No em-dashes. No paragraphs.
Spell out one to nine. Use digits for 10 and above.
Banned: use, use, improve, complete, combined effect, very, really, quite, extremely, incredibly, basically, simply.
Define abbreviations at first use. Use category names not brand names.

## Publishing Rules

- Append-only: never delete documents, deprecate with status: deprecated
- Numbers are permanent: cross-references must never break
- ASCII-only: no Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Cyrillic, accented characters
- Abbreviation first-use: spell out on first occurrence
