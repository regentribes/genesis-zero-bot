# Circular Economy And Waste Streaming Closed Loop Resource Management

Circular economy eliminates the concept of waste by treating all outputs as inputs to other processes.

Waste streaming separates waste into distinct material flows that can be recaptured and reused.

The goal is closing material loops at every scale from household to industrial.

Zero waste targets aim to divert all materials from landfill through reduction, reuse, and recycling.

Industrial symbiosis connects multiple processes so that the waste output of one becomes the raw material input of another.

Nutrient cycling returns organic matter to the soil, mimicking the forest floor.

Organ (metaphor for a functional subsystem or team):
Organic waste streams include food scraps, garden waste, and human waste.

Processing pathways include composting for soil amendment, biodigestion for biogas production, and constructed wetlands for water treatment.

Outputs include compost for gardens, biogas for cooking and heating, and nutrient-rich water for irrigation.

Inorganic waste streams include metals, plastics, glass, and electronics.

Processing pathways include material recovery facilities, repair workshops, and additive manufacturing for recycling plastic into new products.

Material passports document the composition and provenance of materials in products and buildings.

Passports enable recovery and reuse at end of life by revealing what materials are present and where.

Digital passports stored on blockchain ensure tamper-proof records that persist through ownership changes.

A regenerative community implements comprehensive waste streaming.

All organic waste becomes compost or biogas.

All recyclable materials are processed within the community or regional network.

Construction materials are designed for disassembly with material passports enabling future reuse.

The community achieves true zero waste by ensuring no material is discarded.

With Ecology, composting builds soil health and sequesters carbon.

With Hardware, waste heat from data centers warms buildings.

With Economy, material recovery creates jobs and reduces input costs.

With Software, blockchain tracks material passports through the supply chain.
