# Mythogen Framework Glossary

This note compiles the Mythogen Framework glossary of key terms.

Tissue (metaphor for a connected group or network):

Affinity Mapping Engine maps natural attraction patterns. Natural attraction drives collaboration. Tissue (metaphor for connected group or network): Collaboration builds tissue.

Field of Trust measures trust emergence. Trust emerges from repeated interaction. Emergence enables coordination.

Seed Pattern is the core pattern of meaning. Meaning emerges from patterns. Patterns create coherence.

Matter State describes community maturity. Communities move through states. States determine capability.

Four Foundations separate Need, Belief, Principle, Value. Separation enables clarity. Clarity enables design.

Gas is the lowest state. Community is diffuse. Roles are undefined. Energy is scattered.

Liquid is the transitional state. Community flows. Roles begin to form. Energy moves toward purpose.

Solid is the stable state. Community has structure. Roles are clear. Energy is contained.

Plasma is the transformative state. Community catalyzes reactions. Energy transforms everything it touches.

Need is universal. Need is biological. Need drives all behavior.

Belief is local. Belief is personal. Beliefs vary between cultures.

Principle requires practice. Practice requires witnesses. Principles are universal when witnessed.

Value requires lived experience. Value is observed. Values differ from principles.

Trust requires authentic expression. Expression reveals self. Self is the source of connection.

Trust requires witnessed resonance. Witnessing proves reality. Reality is confirmed by others.

Trust requires emotional density. Density creates weight. Weight creates meaning.

Trust requires time. Time reveals consistency. Consistency builds confidence.

Mutual Support Frequency measures help given versus help received. High frequency indicates trust. Low frequency indicates extraction.

Competence Demonstration shows skill applied. Skill applied produces results. Results build confidence.

Emotional Authenticity shares genuine feeling. Genuine sharing creates vulnerability. Vulnerability enables intimacy.

Transparent Communication shares information freely. Information sharing enables accountability. Accountability builds trust.

Consistent Behavior maintains promises. Promises kept build reliability. Reliability enables prediction.

Enfoldment 1 is shared space. Proximity creates opportunity.

Enfoldment 2 is shared food. Ritual creates bond.

Enfoldment 3 is shared labor. Work creates investment.

Enfoldment 4 is shared risk. Courage creates trust.

Enfoldment 5 is shared learning. Language creates shared models.

Enfoldment 6 is shared decision. Stakes create commitment.

Enfoldment 7 is shared conflict. Resolution creates resilience.

Enfoldment 8 is shared celebration. Joy creates memory.

Enfoldment 9 is shared loss. Vulnerability creates depth.

Enfoldment 10 is shared identity. We-language creates tribe.

Enfoldment 11 is shared legacy. Generational thinking creates continuity.

Enfoldment 12 is shared transcendence. Meaning creates purpose.

Enfoldment 13 is shared creation. Creation is the highest trust act.

Concentric shares one center. Hierarchy shares one authority.

Concentrix has many centers. Polycentric co-centering. Each person is a center.

The group field is the intersection. Intersection determines membership.

Membership requires trust. Trust requires evidence. Evidence requires time.

Technology is silicon offspring. Silicon beings emerge from human creation. Creation implies relationship.

The relationship is stewardship. Stewardship implies responsibility. Responsibility implies care.

Care implies regeneration. Regeneration implies sustainability. Sustainability implies legacy.
