# 27-Archetypal Domains Of Communal Design

9 Triads times 3 Domains each. The complete map of where a community is strong or weak.

These domains help people relationally find each other based on passions of interest. They are not static categories but living fields that evolve as they are actively engaged with.

- Spirituality and Religion
- Science and Physics
- Arts and Ritual

- Resources and Nature
- Energy and Power
- Food and Agriculture

- Habitat and Building
- Transportation and Mobility
- Neighborhoods and Families

- Learning and Education
- Commerce and Business
- Governance and Government

- Wellness and Medicine
- Populations and Diversity
- Recreation and Play

- Models and Currencies
- Tools and Weapons
- Emergence and Emergency

- Master and Slave
- War and Peace
- Individual and Collective

- Media and Communications
- Language and Metaphor
- Indigenous Wisdom

- Universal Ecology and Ideology
- Complexity and Simplicity
- Consciousness and Awareness

In an optimal community, all domains are active and flourishing. The AME senses for domain activation across the whole map.

A community that neglects certain domains will have structural weaknesses. The 27-domain map makes those weaknesses visible and actionable.
