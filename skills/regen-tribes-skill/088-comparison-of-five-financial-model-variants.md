# Comparison Of Five Financial Model Variants

This note captures the comprehensive comparison of five financial model variants from the RegenTribes Telegram conversation (Apr 2026).

Variant 1 operates as an unincorporated association. This provides legal standing without corporate complexity.

Variant 2 forms as a cooperative. This provides formal governance structure and member rights.

Variant 3 establishes as a nonprofit. This provides tax advantages and grant eligibility.

Variant 4 registers as a DA0. This provides smart contract enforcement and pseudonymous participation.

Variant 5 functions as a natural law entity. This provides sovereignty claims and legal ambiguity.

System (metaphor for an integrated whole or network):

Variant 1 uses mutual credit. Credit issued by members for members. No external money required.

Variant 2 uses time banking. One hour equals one hour. Equal exchange regardless of skill.

Variant 3 uses energy accounting. Joules measured. Work valued by energy content.

Variant 4 uses cryptocurrency. Bitcoin or Ethereum. Volatility managed by reserves.

Variant 5 uses gift economy. No formal exchange. Contributions flow from abundance.

Variant 1 uses consent decision making. No objection means proceed. Objection requires resolution.

Variant 2 uses sociocracy. Circles within circles. Double-linked roles.

Variant 3 uses holacracy. Glassfrog implementation. Explicit roles and accountabilities.

Variant 4 uses token voting. One token one vote. Or weighted by tenure.

Variant 5 uses rotating facilitation. No permanent roles. Facilitation rotates by function.

Variant 1 allocates by affinity. Work flows to natural attraction. No central budgeting.

Tissue (metaphor for a connected group or network):
Variant 2 allocates by planning. Tissues propose. Federation approves. Resources distributed by priority.

Variant 3 allocates by algorithm. SurrealDB queries production. Matching allocates consumption.

Variant 4 allocates by market. Prices emerge from supply and demand. Adjustments automatic.

Variant 5 allocates by needs. Basic needs first. Surplus available for expansion.

Variant 1 trades with other tissues. Trade uses agreed currency. Exchange rate by negotiation.

Variant 2 trades with conventional economy. Surplus sold. Imports purchased. Exchange rate by market.

Variant 3 restricts external trade. Priority is internal circulation. External trade is supplement.

Variant 4 integrates with crypto economy. Defi protocols. Cross-chain bridges. Liquidity pools.

Variant 5 avoids external trade. Closed loop preferred. External contact minimal.

Variant 1 forms capital through labor credit. Labor credit accumulates. Capital forms from surplus labor.

Variant 2 forms capital through surplus allocation. Percentage of production to common fund.

Variant 3 forms capital through energy surplus. Energy beyond needs converted to stored value.

Variant 4 forms capital through token issuance. Governance tokens sold. Capital raised for projects.

Variant 5 forms capital through gift. Members gift surplus. Capital forms from accumulated gifts.

Variant 1 uses restorative circles. Harm acknowledged. Repair negotiated. Relationships restored.

Variant 2 uses majority consent. Minority agrees to defer. Deferral rotates by issue.

Variant 3 uses veto with compensation. Vetoes possible. Compensation paid for exercise. Vetoes rare.

Variant 4 uses arbitration. Neutral party decides. Decision binding.

Variant 5 uses dissolution option. Members can exit with proportional share. Exit forces negotiation.

Variant 1 uses FOT tracking. Trust field visible. Low trust limits participation.

Variant 2 uses transparency dashboards. All transactions public. All decisions recorded.

Variant 3 uses smart contract logs. Every action logged. Every log immutable.

Variant 4 uses reputation systems. Ratings published. Rankings visible.

Variant 5 uses oral history. Reputation carried by community. Memory is the record.

Each variant improves for different values.
Variant 1 improves for autonomy.
Variant 2 improves for coordination.
Variant 3 improves for efficiency.
Variant 4 improves for liquidity.
Variant 5 improves for simplicity.

The choice depends on context. Context determines which value dominates.

Tissue (metaphor for connected group or network): A single tissue may implement one variant. A federation may implement multiple variants. Federation enables choice. Choice enables adaptation.
