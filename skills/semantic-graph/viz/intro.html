How It Works

When you feed documents into the graph, it:

  1. Extracts key ideas — pulls out the important concepts
  2. Maps relationships — figures out how ideas connect
  3. Understands meaning mathematically — so it can find related concepts even when different words are used

How to Use It

📥 Ingest (Add Knowledge)

Run the ingest script on any document (PDF, text, links) to add it to the graph.

🔍 Query (Ask Questions)

Ask things like "what relates to regenerative water?" or "how does governance connect to land stewardship?"

What It's Good At

  • 🔗 Finding unexpected connections between ideas
  • 🧭 Exploring what the community knows about a topic
  • 🔄 Cross-referencing concepts across different sources
  • ✨ Sparking serendipitous discoveries

Limitations

❌ Can't recall exact quotes 📄 Doesn't store full documents 🧠 Can't guess what you don't know to ask 🌫️ Loses nuance and hedging

Examples

  • ✅
    "What's the relationship between circular economy and gift economy?"
    → Shows the hidden links between them
  • ✅
    "What do you know about water systems?"
    → Pulls all related concepts
  • ❌
    "What exact quote did the author use?"
    → Can't do this (not stored)
