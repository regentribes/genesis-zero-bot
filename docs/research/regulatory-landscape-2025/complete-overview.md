### Rec 49: Physical Internet
- **Purpose:** Create global logistics standard for modular, intermodal container shipping
- **Key elements:** 600x400mm modular containers, global standard, smart containers with sensors, digital twin
- **Status:** Pilot phase in Europe and Asia
- **Relevance:** Combined with EU DPP = complete transparency layer for community goods

### Rec 50: Circular Economy Trade
- **Purpose:** Enable trade in secondary materials, reverse logistics, circular economy
- **Key elements:** Harmonized definitions for recycled content, traceability of material cycles, standards for refurbishment/remanufacture, cross-border waste movement
- **Status:** Recently adopted, implementation starting
- **Relevance:** Required for EU DPP Article 9 compliance

## Regional DPP Landscape

### EU Digital Product Passport (ESPR)
- **Launch:** July 2026 (EU Registry)
- **Applies to:** Nearly all physical products
- **Priority:** Batteries (2026), textiles (2027), electronics (2027-2030)
- **Data elements:** Origin/provenance, environmental footprint, repairability, end-of-life, supply chain actors
- **Architecture:** Decentralized, QR code links to EU registry, UNTP Core Vocabulary

### Japan
- **Textile traceability:** QR codes on care labels, links to online platform
- **DX Promotion Act:** Digital transformation mandate, supply chain data sharing incentives, SME digitization subsidies
- **Implication:** Japanese brands (UNIQLO, Fast Retailing) require supply chain transparency from suppliers

### South Korea
- **2025 developments:** Mandatory recycled content, resource circularity reporting, right to repair (proposed)
- **EU alignment:** Korean exporters must comply with EU DPP
- **Eco-Design Cooperation Forum:** July 2025 meeting to align with EU standards
- **ESG reporting:** IFRS S1/S2 alignment, supply chain disclosure

### UK
- **Product Security:** Smart product labeling, IoT device security certifications
- **Post-Brexit:** UK equivalent of ESPR in development, divergence from EU
- **Textiles:** Extended Producer Responsibility, mandatory recycled content

### US
- **No federal DPP yet:** State-level patchwork (CA, NY)
- **EPA initiatives:** National Recycling Strategy, proposed recycled content rules
- **Implication:** Compliance complexity for multi-market sellers

### China
- **CECC Platform:** QR codes on all imported goods, links to customs registration
- **Quality traceability:** Batch-level tracking for food, cosmetics, electronics
- **Green labeling:** Environmental certification required for EU exports

## Global Actors

### PYX Protocol (pyx.io)
- **Type:** Decentralized identity + reputation protocol
- **Functions:** Self-sovereign identity, reputation scores, verifiable credentials, social graph
- **Technical basis:** Ethereum (ERC-725/735), Graph protocol, IPFS
- **Status:** Early stage development, not as mature as ONCHAINID
- **For RegenTribes:** Could replace ONCHAINID for member ID, reputation tracking

### RBA — Responsible Business Alliance
- **Scale:** World's largest industry coalition for supply chain responsibility
- **Members:** Apple, Dell, Samsung, Intel, HP, Cisco, Microsoft, Sony, Nike, etc.
- **Coverage:** 100+ countries, millions of workers
- **Code v8.0.1 (2025):** Labor, Health & Safety, Environment, Ethics, Management Systems

### RBA VAP Audit
- **Recognition levels:** Platinum (200+), Gold (180+), Silver (160+)
- **Findings:** Priority, Major, Minor severity
- **Auditors:** Bureau Veritas, SGS, DNV, TUV SUD, UL
- **Process:** Document review, interviews, site inspection (1-6 days)
- **Corrective actions:** CAP required, closure assessments
