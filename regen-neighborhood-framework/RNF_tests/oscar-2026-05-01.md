## Vision

**One-liner:** Re-develop society

**What you're building:**
A regenerative neighborhood with resilient systems for housing, water, food, energy, economy, ecology, human health & connection. Designed as a **destination village** with a significant visitor economy.

**For whom:**
Multigenerational community — singles and families — regenerative mindsets, growth-minded, emotionally intelligent humans who want to collaborate to make awesome businesses, art, and experiences.

**Core values:**
- Land held in common (never privatized)
- Decisions follow governance layers (sociocracy + meritocracy + democracy + AI advisor)
- Zero waste — solutions for all streams
- Universal participation — everyone can earn decision-making abilities
- Contributors rewarded by income that covers costs + generates income
- Nature as partner, included in income share

## Governance Stack

Multi-layer system:
1. **Sociocracy (circles/departments)** — operations, domain autonomy
2. **Meritocracy** — expert decisions
3. **Democracy** — one person one vote, strategic/veto layer
4. **Benevolent AI advisor** — decision engine trained on documented values, principles, rules, and decisions; activates for verification when human layers need it

**Verification rule:** AI + 3 layers of human voting confirm when necessary.

## Economic Model

**Core principle:** Community generates income; contributors receive income that covers their costs + generates profit.

**Visitor economy revenue streams:**
- Day visitor guest passes + experiences
- Retreat packages + premium amenity access
- Events, food, workshops

**Implied:** Some form of membership contribution tracking, revenue sharing, and possibly tokenized contribution accounting.

**Open questions:**
- What is the business model? (Social enterprise? Membership fees? Community-owned enterprises?)
- What are the revenue streams? (Food production? Events? Education? Consulting?)
- How is "costs covered + income generated" quantified per member?
