<b>THE TOP 10 PRIORITY GAPS</b>
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Ranked by: dependency × impact ÷ difficulty

---

<b>#1 — Skill.md format canonical spec</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Enables all other agent work. Without canonical format, no two agents can share skills. This is the foundation.
<i>Enables:</i> Every other agent development gap.

---

<b>#2 — ITC ledger software spec</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Core economic mechanism. Credits issued for contribution, extinguish on use. Everything flows from the credit system.
<i>Enables:</i> Gaps 16, 17, 18, 19

---

<b>#3 — FOT measurement formalism</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Trust infrastructure. Without formal model (Gap 6), reputation is vibes. With it → governance weighting, behavioral signals.
<i>Enables:</i> Gaps 7, 8, 9, 10

---

<b>#4 — Community land trust legal formation</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Physical anchor. Land = permanence. No land = everything is precarious and dependent on extractive systems.
<i>Enables:</i> Gaps 11, 15

---

<b>#5 — LoRa mesh sensor network protocol</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Communications resilience. Without this, community depends on extractive telecom infrastructure.
<i>Enables:</i> Gap 13, real-time data for digital twin

---

<b>#6 — Non-accumulative credit mechanism</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Prevents ITC from becoming money. Without decay/enforcement, ITC inherits all extractive dynamics of currency.
<i>Enables:</i> Economic health of entire ITC system

---

<b>#7 — AME correlation algorithm spec</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Knowledge system core. Without this, the concept graph is a static lookup, not a living intelligence.
<i>Enables:</i> Gap 22, dynamic affinity mapping

---

<b>#8 — Agent memory persistence cross-session</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Required for continuity. Without this, Genesis is permanently amnesiac between sessions.
<i>Enables:</i> Long-term agent memory, institutional knowledge

---

<b>#9 — Consent-based governance + smart contract</b>
<i>Why first:</i> CDS implementation. Without this, cooperative governance is just conversation.
<i>Enables:</i> Gap 2, legitimate cooperative decision-making

---

<b>#10 — Microgrid islanding</b>
<i>Why first:</i> Energy sovereignty. Without this, community dies when the grid goes down.
<i>Enables:</i> Gap 14, community survival
