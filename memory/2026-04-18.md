## Article continued: Old Roots, New Shoots (full)

Key frameworks from the article:

**Tribes:**
- "Sangha" = intentional community devoted to practice of being together (not just shared outcomes)
- Tribe = emotional hearth, counter to loneliness AND commodification of belonging
- Conscious tribe choice = challenge to nationalism, consumerism, dogmatism
- Future tribes = social immune systems, crucibles of collective effervescence

**Collectives:**
- Fluid swarms for project economy
- Open-source model: anyone contribute, reputation earned, projects independent of founders
- Accountability gap = main risk
- Quadratic voting, consensus protocols, smart contracts for resource sharing

**Cooperatives:**
- Platform co-ops = Uber/Airbnb owned by workers/users
- Blockchain + decentralized governance + legal hacks = spread ownership
- Bureaucracy + scalability = ongoing challenges

**Guilds:**
- Neo-guilds: learning, mentorship, community standards, insurance
- Audax case study: meta-guild with tokenized participation + reputation + Web3 + craft/standards/belonging
- Gatekeeping risk = dark side
- "Seal of approval could actually mean something again"

**Venture Family:**
- Orgs as interconnected family members, not isolated silos
- Pool knowledge, infrastructure, talent
- Trust + aligned purpose over transactional relationships

**AI as Glue:**
- Onboarding new members
- Collective intelligence platforms
- Conflict mediation
- Dynamic adaptation to group needs

**Pitfalls (what to watch for):**
- Tribes → exclusivity, "us vs them"
- Collectives → accountability gaps, diffuse responsibility
- Co-ops → bureaucracy, endless meetings
- Guilds → elitism, gatekeeping

**Core thesis:**
- Future = constellation of communities (tribe for belonging, collective for activism, co-op for economics, guild for craft)
- NOT one right model — patchwork that meets full range of human needs
- Human-scale > network state fantasy
- No tech substitutes for trust, shared story, actual relationships
