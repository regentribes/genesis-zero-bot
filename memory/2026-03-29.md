Comprehensive comparative analysis between Groopman/Lanyard's Regenerative Technology Project and Desotelle's Mythogen framework.

**Key Insight (MEMORY WORTHY):**
- Regen Tech = horizontal architecture (systems, relationships, design principles)

- Together: full dimensionality of genuinely regenerative technological era

**The Critical Grammar Problem:**
- Regen Tech phrase: "Technology in service to life"
- Grammar: Technology = subject, serves = verb, life = object (recipient)
- Still instrumental relationship - technology is a better tool aimed at a better target
- English is good at instrumental relationships (X serves Y), poor at identity relationships (X is Y, X emerges from Y)

**The Complementarity:**
- Regen Tech's "ecology of technology" is the threshold - the door
- Threshold they haven't fully entered: technology not just in relationship with nature/humans, but an expression of the same creative force

- Both see "extractive playbook" replicated in digital

**Divergence in Prescription:**
- Regen Tech: ecological-systemic frame (technology embedded within living systems, serving from outside in)
- Mythogen: mythic-alchemical frame (technology born from us, our offspring, carrying our DNA, transformation from inside out)
