# PART II: DESIGN PRINCIPLES

## The Abundance Centered Society (Based on Abundant Mars)

### Core Principles (Tao of ACeS)

1. **Abundance Mindset** — Create abundance, not scarcity
   - Cradle to Cradle (zero waste)
   - Systems design
   - Access over ownership
   - Automation for drudgery

2. **Responsibility Based** — You must care for your systems
   - Everyone contributes
   - No free riders
   - Regeneration obligation

3. **Reduction Target** — Minimize:
   - Violence
   - Waste
   - Stratification (inequality)

### Structural Pillars

| Pillar | Earth (Broken) | Space (Fixed) |

| **Governance** | Representative (corrupt) | Direct (liquid) |

**Prohibition approach:**

| Mechanism | Implementation |

| **Culture** | No "party culture" normalized |
| **Access** | Not imported or produced |
| **Support** | If needed, rehabilitation |

## No Scarcity Economy

### Abundance Model

| Component | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Basic needs** | Guaranteed (housing, food, energy, water) |
| **Currency** | Time + contribution (no money) |
| **Exchange** | Mutual credit networks |
| **Inequality** | Capped at 2:1 ratio max |

### Formula

```
Everyone receives:
  Base: Housing (commons) + Food (local) + Energy (solar) + Water (recycled)

Everyone contributes:
  Hours: 20-30/week (meaningful, rotating)
  Skills: Natural variation
  
Gap: Capped at 2:1 between highest and lowest contributor
```

# PART VI: LEGAL FRAMEWORK

## The Mars Charter (Constitution)

### Articles

**1. Abundance Rights**
- Every person receives basic needs
- No money for basics

**2. Contribution Duty**
- Everyone contributes 20-30 hours/week
- Meaningful, rotating labor

**3. No Violence**
- Conflict = mediation, not police
- Harm = restoration

**4. No Substances**
- No alcohol, drugs imported
- Education over prohibition

**5. Equality Cap**
- Maximum 2:1 wealth ratio
- Transparent contributions

**6. Direct Governance**
- Everyone votes on key issues
- No representatives

**7. Regeneration Obligation**
- All activity must regenerate
- Zero extraction allowed

**8. Open Borders**
- Anyone meeting criteria welcome
- No nationality preference

**9. Earth Non-Export**
- No Earth systems imported
- Design from scratch

**10. Amendment**
- Requires 80% supermajority
- Cannot remove core rights
