# Regenerative Neighborhood Framework (RNF)
## Complete Framework Structure & Implementation Guide

---

## PART 1: FRAMEWORK ARCHITECTURE

### Core Framework Principle

**Ecology is the operating system — everything else is an application.**

---

## THE 5-PILLAR STRUCTURE (Nested/Concentric)

### 1. ECOLOGY (Foundation)
Climate & environmental systems — the base layer everything else builds upon.
- Climate patterns and weather tracking
- Environmental monitoring systems
- GHG emissions management
- Regenerative agriculture climate alignment
- Water cycle management
- Temperature regulation strategies

### 2. HARDWARE (Built Environment)
Physical infrastructure — buildings, energy, transport.
- Energy systems (solar, wind, storage, grid)
- Water systems (collection, filtration, recycling)
- Buildings & housing solutions
- Transportation infrastructure
- Communications infrastructure

### 3. HUMAN SYSTEMS (Community & Governance)
Relationships, safety, learning, stewardship.
- Community governance models
- Decision-making frameworks
- Education & skill-sharing
- Health & wellness systems
- Safety & security structures
- Cultural & creative expression

### 4. ECONOMY (Circular Systems)
Resource circulation and value exchange.
- Circular economy principles
- Local currency & exchange systems
- Labor & contribution tracking
- Resource sharing models
- Investment & capital pathways

### 5. SOFTWARE (Knowledge & Digital)
Tools, knowledge, communication.
- Knowledge management systems
- Communication platforms
- Data & analytics tools
- Planning & design tools

---

## PART 2: IMPLEMENTATION PATHWAYS

### The Hybrid Sequencing Model (Spirals)

The RNF uses a spiral-based development model with 5 phases:

1. **Feasibility Validation Spiral** — Site assessment, initial design, group alignment
2. **Proof-of-Concept Spiral** — Pilot projects, small-scale implementations
3. **Capital Optimization Spiral** — Funding, investment, resource mobilization
4. **Construction & Activation Spiral** — Buildout, systems integration
5. **Deep Design Phase** — Final master planning, detailed specs

### Gate System

- **Gate 1**: Group Alignment Confirmed
- **Gate 2**: Land Secured
- **Gate 3**: Preliminary Master Plan Approved
- **Gate 4**: Capital Secured
- **Gate 5**: Full Master Plan Complete
